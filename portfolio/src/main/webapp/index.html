About me

I was born in Arizona, and I grew up in a family of six, with three older brothers. Growing up the son of two immigrants, I learned how to budget my little time and resources to work hard at what I prioritized, something I learned from watching my parents who budgeted their time and resources in order to provide me and my brothers an education and constantly care for those around them. These are things that I practice each day, striving to work hard in every task I'm given and to help as many people as I can, and above all, remaining grateful for all the opportunities and privileges I've been granted.
